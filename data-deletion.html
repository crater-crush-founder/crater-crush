Data Deletion Request

Last updated: April 2026

This page explains how you can request deletion of your data associated with Crater Crush.

What data is involved

Crater Crush may process limited data such as:

  • Your Google Play Games profile name
  • Your player ID
  • Leaderboard scores

How to request deletion

To request deletion of your data, contact us via email:

cratercrushgame@gmail.com

Please include:

  • Your Google Play Games nickname
  • A description of your request

What happens after your request

We will review your request and remove associated leaderboard data where possible.

Some data managed by third-party services (such as Google Play Games) must be handled through those services directly.

Alternative option

You can also clear app data via your device settings to remove locally stored and cloud-stored game progress information.
