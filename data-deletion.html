Crater Crush

Account and Data Deletion

Last updated: April 17, 2026

This page explains how you can request deletion of your Crater Crush app account data and other associated data.

1. What this request covers

Crater Crush uses Google Play Games Services for sign-in and uses PlayFab for leaderboard-related data. A deletion request applies to app-related data controlled by Crater Crush, not to your Google account itself.

2. Data that may be deleted

Depending on your request, Crater Crush may delete app-related data such as:

  • PlayFab leaderboard entries associated with your player profile
  • Other app-side data controlled directly by Crater Crush, where applicable
  • Locally stored data and Google cloud save data when you use the in-game reset/delete option

3. Data not deleted by this request

The following are not deleted by Crater Crush directly:

  • Your Google account
  • Your Google Play Games profile
  • Data managed solely by Google outside the controls available to the app

If you want to manage or delete Google account data or Google Play Games profile information, you must do so through Google’s own account and service settings.

4. In-game deletion option

Crater Crush includes an in-game option that deletes local progress data and Google cloud-stored progress associated with your save data. This in-game option does not currently delete PlayFab leaderboard data.

5. How to request deletion

To request deletion of PlayFab leaderboard data or other applicable app-controlled data, contact:

cratercrushgame@gmail.com

Please include the following, if available:

  • Your Google Play Games nickname
  • Any player ID or identifying details that help locate your data
  • A short description of what you want deleted

6. How requests are handled

Requests are reviewed manually. After verification, applicable app-controlled data will be deleted where reasonably possible.

7. Retention

If a deletion request requires temporary retention for fraud prevention, dispute handling, legal compliance, or technical recovery processing, the relevant data may be retained only for as long as reasonably necessary for that purpose.

8. Contact

For account or data deletion requests, contact:
cratercrushgame@gmail.com
